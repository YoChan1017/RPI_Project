Raspberry Pi          MCP3208             MQ-3 Sensor
=============         =========           ===========
3.3V  (PIN 1)   -->   VDD, VREF
GND   (PIN 15)   -->   AGND, DGND
GPIO11 (PIN 23) -->   CLK
GPIO10 (PIN 19) -->   DIN
GPIO9  (PIN 21) -->   DOUT
GPIO8  (PIN 24) -->   CS/SHDN

5V    (PIN 4)   -->   MQ-3 VCC
GND   (PIN 6)   -->   MQ-3 GND
MCP3208 CH0     <--   MQ-3 A0
